What is VAANI?

VAANI - A Gamified Vocal Training System, a multi-purpose system based on pitch detection of audio signals and accomplishing pattern matching, thus dispensing a resemblance score and aiding novice singers to rectify their inaccuracies.

The major algorithmic modules, therefore, are pitch estimation and melodic similarity distance computation.
